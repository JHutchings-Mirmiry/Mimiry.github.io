Streamline Your Hospitality Workflows with Zapier’s No-Code AI Automation

For UK hospitality SMEs, managing daily operations—bookings, guest inquiries, staff schedules—can be overwhelming. Manual processes lead to errors and eat into time better spent on guest experiences. Zapier is a no-code AI automation platform that solves this by connecting your apps and automating workflows. Freeing you to focus on what matters. At Mimiry, we can integrate Zapier to transform how your businesses operates.

The Workflow Challenge

Hotels, restaurants, and pubs juggle multiple tools: booking systems, CRMs, and email platforms. Coordinating these manually is inefficient. For example, updating a booking in your PMS and then emailing the guest takes minutes per transaction. Multiply that by dozens daily, and it’s hours lost. Zapier bridges these gaps, automating tasks across 7,000+ apps.

How Zapier Works

Zapier creates “Zaps” automated workflows triggered by events. For instance:
• Booking Automation: A new reservation in your PMS (e.g., Cloudbeds) triggers an email confirmation via Gmail and adds the guest to your CRM (e.g., HubSpot).
• Staff Scheduling: A Google Calendar shift update notifies staff via WhatsApp and logs hours in a Google Sheet.
• Review Management: A positive TripAdvisor review prompts a thank you email and adds the guest to a loyalty campaign.
Zapier’s AI features, like text parsing and lead scoring, enhance these workflows. For example, it can prioritize high value bookings for follow ups. 

Benefits for UK SMEs

• Time Savings: Automate 50% of repetitive tasks, saving 5-10 hours weekly.
• Error Reduction: Eliminate manual data entry mistakes, ensuring accurate guest records.
• Affordability: Plans start at £15/month, with no coding required.
• Scalability: Add Zaps as your business grows, integrating with GDPR-compliant tools.
