The Operational Burden

For small hotels and restaurants, manual back-office work is a bottleneck. Sorting guest inquiries, tracking stock, or categorizing expenses can take hours daily. Errors in these tasks lead to overstocking, missed payments, or delayed responses. Levity uses AI to classify, tag, and process data automatically, streamlining operations.

How Levity Works

Levity’s interface lets you train AI models without technical skills. Key applications include:

• Email Sorting: Automatically categorize guest emails (e.g., bookings, complaints) and route them to the right team.
• Inventory Management: Tag and track stock levels from supplier emails, alerting you to reorder.
• Document Processing: Extract data from invoices or contracts, logging it into your accounting software. 

Levity integrates with tools like Airtable and Google Sheets, ensuring compatibility with existing systems.
